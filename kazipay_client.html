Scope of Work — Brand Identity Redesign

Deliverables

  • Primary logo — 3 initial concepts, 2 rounds of revision on chosen direction, final files in AI, PNG, SVG, PDF
  • Logo variations — horizontal lockup, stacked, icon-only, mono versions
  • Colour palette — primary, secondary, accent colours with HEX, RGB, CMYK and Pantone values
  • Typography — heading and body typeface selection with usage guidelines
  • Brand guidelines — 12-16 page PDF covering logo usage, clear space, colour, typography, do's and don'ts
  • Business cards — front and back design, print-ready files
  • Social media templates — 5 templates for Instagram (posts and stories) in Canva-editable format
  • Stationery — letterhead and envelope design, print-ready

Timeline

Week 1: Research, moodboard, initial concepts
Week 2: Refinement, brand system development
Week 3: Final deliverables, handover

Revisions

2 rounds of revisions included. Additional rounds billed at KES 3,000 per round.
